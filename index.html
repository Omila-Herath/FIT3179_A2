Metropolitan Melbourne Property Sales (2016-2017)

This heat map allows us to explore the frequency of property sales in Metropolitan Melbourne with respect to the building area and land size. Use the slider to filter by maximum sale price.

This choropleth map is displays Metropolitan Melbourne suburbs, using colour to represent variations in the average property land size. It illustrates spatial patterns and differences in property land sizes across the Metropolitan area. Each suburb is shaded according to its respective average land size, with darker colors indicating larger properties, while lighter colours represent smaller ones.

It is evident that in general, properties are larger, the further they are from the Melbourne CBD. This is due to the fact that land is cheaper at these further distances because of lower demand, and because some of these properties (especially very far from the CBD) are farm-land.

This choropleth should be contrasted with the sale price choropleth (above), as it shows that in general, Melbournians pay more for smaller properties, and conversely less for larger properties.
