Causes of Death around the world from 1990 - 2019

This Area map displays the deaths caused by various diseases globally

- Select a cause of death from the drop down option to see it's impact on global deaths

The bar Graph represents the total sum of deaths in each continent for the selected cause of death from the time periods 1990 - 2019
- Select from the Drop down menu a cause of death to see it's impact on each continent

The line graph represents the total deaths per continent from the time periods 1990 - 2019

In general, there is a decreasing death trend for many diseases which can be attributed to advancedments in medical techniques. HIV-AIDS for example has a steep increase in deaths around 2002 - 2004 within Africa but decreases significantly as it approaches 2019

Deaths caused by Tuberculosis

This choropleth map displays the deaths caused by Tuberculosis over time. Interestingly we can see that over time, the deaths in india remain at a relatively high level whilst in general the amount of deaths decrease over time

* Move the slider to see how the deaths caused by Tuberculosis changes

This Pie chart represents the Deaths caused by Tuberculosis per Continent over time.

We can see that majority of the deaths occoured in Asia, followed closely by Africa. This could likely be due to medical products not being readily accesible
